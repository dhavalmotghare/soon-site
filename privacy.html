Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Soon ("the app") is built with privacy as a default. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data we collect

None. Soon does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics SDKs, no crash-reporting services, and no advertising networks.

All event data you create (names, dates, categories) is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. It never leaves your device.

Permissions

Notifications

Soon requests permission to send notifications in order to deliver optional event reminders (7 days before, 1 day before, and on the day of each event at 9 AM). These reminders are scheduled locally using Android's AlarmManager — no external server is involved. You can disable them at any time from the app's Settings screen or from your device's notification settings.

Calendar

Soon does not request the READ_CALENDAR or WRITE_CALENDAR permissions. The "Add to calendar" feature works by launching your device's system calendar app via a standard Android Intent, pre-filled with the event details. The calendar app handles the save — Soon never reads or writes to your calendar directly.

Third-party services

Soon uses no third-party SDKs or services that collect data. The app uses the following open-source libraries solely for local functionality:

  • Jetpack Compose — UI rendering
  • Jetpack Glance — home screen widget
  • Room — local database
  • Inter typeface (bundled) — typography

None of these libraries transmit any data off your device.

Data storage & security

All event data is stored in a Room (SQLite) database in your app's private storage. This storage is only accessible to the Soon app and is deleted when you uninstall the app. We have no access to this data.

Children's privacy

Soon does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. As the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change in a future version, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.

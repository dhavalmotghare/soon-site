Count down to
what matters.

Soon turns your upcoming events into a living visual rhythm — not just a number, but a sense of time passing.

Features

Core

Semantic bars

Each bar is a real unit of time. The scale adapts automatically — days, weeks, months, or years — so every event feels proportional.

Core

12 categories

Birthday, Travel, Work, Health, Entertainment, Personal, Sports, Education, Finance, Anniversary, Goal, and Event — each with its own colour theme.

Detail

Live countdown

In the final 3 days, the card switches to a live hours : minutes : seconds countdown so you never miss the moment.

Detail

Work arc ring

Work events show a circular progress ring tracking the percentage of total time elapsed — great for project deadlines.

Widget

Home screen widget

A 4×2 Glance widget that mirrors the in-app card. Long-press to configure up to 5 events and tap to cycle through them.

Widget

Dark mode aware

The widget adapts to your system theme instantly. Override to always Light or Dark from Settings and apply it to the widget too.

Actions

Swipe gestures

Swipe left to reveal edit and add-to-calendar actions. Swipe right to delete. Or just tap the card to reveal the options.

Actions

System calendar

Add any event to your device calendar in one tap — no extra permissions needed. Opens pre-filled and ready to save.

Reminders

Push notifications

Optional reminders at 7 days before, 1 day before, and on the day of each event, all delivered at 9 AM.

Tabs

All · Upcoming · Done

All shows every upcoming event. Upcoming filters to within 20 days. Done keeps your past events for reference.
